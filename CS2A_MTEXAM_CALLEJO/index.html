Tourist Spots and Adventures

Burayok Falls(Arangin,santa lucia)

The beauty of Burayok can only be seen during the rainy season as its water disappears during summer.

#Burayokfalls #NatureTrip #EcoTourism #Adventure #BeutyofNature

Bahay na Bato (luna,la union)

Bahay Na Bato is built from pebble stones from Luna's pebbled beaches.You can enjoy the open art gallery and museum for a minimal entrance fee. Bahay na Bato is a place to visit every now and then.

#BahaynaBato #SustainableArt #Creativity #Travel #IlocosSur

Sta. Lucia Church

The majestic Sta. Lucia Church was built by Augustinian friars in 1586. It is the only church in the whole Philippines with a dome look like that of the St. Peter's Basilica in Rome.(SANTUARIO DE SANTA LUCIA)

INABEL

CHOCOLATE TABLEA

Tablea, a traditional Filipino chocolate, is made from ground-up cacao beans, typically heated and combined with water to create a rich beverage known as tsokolate or sikwate.

DUDOL

Dudol is a traditional Ilocano delicacy made from rice flour, coconut milk, and sugarcane juice. It is known for its sticky, sweet texture and is often compared to a toffee-like kakanin

PINAKBET

Pinakbet is a traditional Filipino vegetable dish originating from the Ilocos Region of the Philippines. It consists of a variety of vegetables, such as bitter melon (ampalaya), eggplant, okra, and squash, all simmered in a savory sauce made from bagoong (fermented shrimp paste). The dish is known for its bold flavors and is often served with rice and grilled or fried meat.

₱70.00
